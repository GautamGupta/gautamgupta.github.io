I am glad to announce my first plugin for bbPress, which is Social It. Social It adds a (X)HTML compliant list of social bookmarks below the first post in your forum topics. You can also use it manually and insert it anywhere on your forum. <\/p>\n

Download: http:\/\/bbpress.org\/plugins\/topic\/social-it\/<\/a><\/h3>\n

You have a variety of options to choose from, or to customize the output. The plugin can add the following social bookmarks:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Twitter<\/li>\n
  • Digg<\/li>\n
  • Delicious<\/li>\n
  • StumpleUpon<\/li>\n
  • Reddit<\/li>\n
  • Diigo<\/li>\n
  • YahooBuzz<\/li>\n
  • Technorati<\/li>\n
  • Mixx<\/li>\n
  • Facebook<\/li>\n
  • Myspace<\/li>\n
  • DesignFloat<\/li>\n
  • GoogleBookmarks<\/li>\n
  • Blinklist<\/li>\n
  • Script & Style<\/li>\n
  • LinkedIn<\/li>\n
  • Newsvine<\/li>\n
  • Devmarks<\/li>\n
  • Mister Wrong<\/li>\n
  • Izeby<\/li>\n
  • Tipd<\/li>\n
  • Friendfeed<\/li>\n
  • BlogMarks<\/li>\n
  • Twittley<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    If you do not find your favourite bookmarking site here, please contact me here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

    The plugin can also shorten your permalinks automatically, and gives you a variety of short-urleners to choose from. Moreover, the bookmarks can also be inserted into the feed.<\/p>\n

    This plugin is inspired from the SexyBookmarks plugin for WordPress made by Josh & Norman. It is also compatible with Support Forum plugin<\/a> Made by Aditya Naik & Sam Bauers.<\/p>\n

    Please do not forget to donate<\/a> for the plugin.<\/p>\n
