I’ve travelled around the Sun 16 times now. This particular revolution saw me starting my first YouTube channel, working hard on Discover I, and speaking at a WordCamp.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

What\u00a0I was doing<\/h3>\n
    \n
  1. WordCamp Jabalpur 2011<\/a> – This has been one of the most awesome events that I’ve ever attended. I was invited as a speaker on bbPress. I met many amazing individuals with whom I had wanted to meet for so long.<\/li>\n
  2. CyberFundu<\/a> – A YouTube channel of various cyber-related videos, started with my schoolmates.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    What\u00a0I’ve been doing<\/h3>\n
      \n
    1. DatumDroid – An Android application that I’m developing for IITB’s TechFest Appsurd event.<\/li>\n
    2. AIMUN – Preparing for Amity International Model United Nations, to be held in November.<\/li>\n
    3. Discover I<\/a> – As mentioned in a previous post, this is a project that I’m working on as a part of Youth Power.<\/li>\n
    4. SpaceSet – A project in which we’ve to design a full Space settlement.<\/li>\n
    5. School – CBSE’s CCE has really proven to be exhaustive, than stress relieving.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

      What\u00a0I\u00a0plan to do<\/h3>\n
        \n
      1. Google Code-in ’11<\/a>: I plan to participate\u00a0in this year’s Google Code-in and try to\u00a0make it to the Google campus again!<\/li>\n
      2. TCS IT Wiz: One of the competitions I really like to attend because of the energised\u00a0atmosphere.<\/li>\n
      3. Amity 46 MUN – Our school’s own MUN to be held in December.<\/li>\n
      4. Learn Java, Python and C++.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

        I had also deactivated my Facebook account as that really used to eat up a lot of my time. I changed its password to something that I myself didn’t know. Tried to reactivate it today for my birthday via forgot password and it has asked me to wait for 24 hours – when all the fun would be\u00a0over. When I have access to it again, I am\u00a0probably going to delete it permanently.<\/p>\n

        Future Plans<\/h3>\n

        In India and elsewhere, one really needs to study hard to get into a good university. \u00a0For that I would study more of Physics, Chemistry and Maths (subjects which I like the most) in the following two years. I would still like to continue with a project or two but not at my current\u00a0pace. More things to follow as soon as they hit my mind.
